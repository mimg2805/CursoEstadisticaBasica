Sample Sizes for the Hypothesis Test of the Difference of 2 Independent Population Proportions

Formula

\( n_i = 2({Z_α + Z_β \over ES})^2 \)

\( ES = \) \( {|π_1 - π_2| \over \sqrt{π (1 - π)}} \)

donde

Zα: 1.96 (confidence at 95%)

Zβ: 0.84 (potency at 80%)

π1: population proportion 1

π2: population proportion 2

π: average population proportion (π 1 + π 2 / 2)

n1: sample size 1

n2: sample size 2
