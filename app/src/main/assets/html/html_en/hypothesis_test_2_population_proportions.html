Hypothesis Test for comparing 2 Population Proportions

Formula

\(Z = {p_1 - p_2 \over \sqrt{{pc (1 - pc) \over n_1} + {pc (1 - pc) \over n_2}}} \)

where

n1: size of sample 1

n2: size of sample 2

p1: proportion of sample 1

p2: proportion of sample 2

pc: combined proportion

(p1 n1 + p2 n2) / n1 + n2
