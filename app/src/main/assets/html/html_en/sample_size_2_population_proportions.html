Sample Sizes for the Difference of 2 Population Proportions

Formula

\( n_i = {[{P_1 (1 - P_1)} + {P_2 (1 - P_2)}] ({Z \over E}})^2 \)

where

P1: estimated proportion of population 1

P2: estimated proportion of population 2

Z: confidence level of 95% (1.960)

E: minimum difference in proportions to detect

n1: sample size 1

n2: sample size 2
