Hypothesis Test for comparing 2 Population Means

Formula

\(Z = {\bar{x}_1 - \bar{x}_2 \over \sqrt{{{s_1}^2 \over n_1} + {{s_2}^2 \over n_2}}} \)

where

n1: sample size of group 1

n2: sample size of group 2

x1: mean of group 1

x2: mean of group 2

s1: standard deviation of group 1

s2: standard deviation of group 2
