Sample Sizes for the Hypothesis Test of the Difference of 2 Independent Population Means

Fórmula

\( n_i = 2({Z_α + Z_β \over ES})^2 \)

\( ES = \) \( {|ϻ_1 - ϻ_2| \over S} \)

donde

Zα: 1.96 (confidence at 95%)

Zβ: 0.84 (potency at 80%)

ϻ1: population mean 1

ϻ2: population mean 2

S1: estimated standard deviation of population 1

S2: estimated standard deviation of population 2

S: S1 + S2 / 2

n1: sample size 1

n2: sample size 2
