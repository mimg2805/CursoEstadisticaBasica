Sample Sizes for the Difference of 2 Population Means

Formula

\( n_i = 2 ({Z S \over E})^2 \)

where

S1: estimated standard deviation of population 1

S2: estimated standard deviation of population 2

Z: confidence level of 95% (1.960)

E: minimum difference in means to detect

S: estimated general deviation (S1 + S2 / 2)

n1: sample size of population 1

n2: sample size of population 2
