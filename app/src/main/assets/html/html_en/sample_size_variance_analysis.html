Sample Size per group (ni) in a one-factor ANOVA with groups of equal size

Formula

ni = \( {2 * S^2 * (Z_α + Z_β)^2} \over Δ^2 \)

where

S2: Variance within groups

Zα: 1.96 (confidence at 95%)

Zβ: 0.84 (power at 80%)

Δ: Minimum significant difference between the means of the groups to be detected (effect to be detected)

Note: This formula assumes that the groups are of equal size and that comparisons are made based on a normal distribution of each group on the dependent variable Y.
